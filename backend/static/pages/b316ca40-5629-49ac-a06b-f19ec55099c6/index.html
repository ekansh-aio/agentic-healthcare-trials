Why Consider Joining the REIMAGINE 4 Study?

The REIMAGINE 4 Phase 3a trial is a rigorously designed research study comparing two investigational treatments in adults with Type 2 diabetes who have not achieved adequate blood sugar control on metformin. Below are key reasons why eligible participants choose to take part.

🆓

No Cost to You

All study-related medical examinations, laboratory tests, investigational medications, and clinical assessments are provided at absolutely no cost to participants for the duration of the trial.

🩺

Close Medical Supervision

Participants receive regular health monitoring from a dedicated clinical team throughout the study, including HbA1c measurements, cardiovascular assessments, and continuous safety evaluations.

🔬

Advance Diabetes Science

By participating, you contribute directly to Phase 3a research that could improve treatment options for the estimated 537 million adults worldwide living with diabetes. Your involvement has lasting scientific value.

💉

Once-Weekly Injectable Treatment

The investigational treatments under evaluation in this study are administered once weekly by injection, offering a potentially convenient dosing schedule for participants with busy lifestyles.

📋

Transparent Informed Consent

Before enrolling, you will receive a full explanation of the study design, your rights as a participant, potential risks and benefits, and all procedures involved. Participation is entirely voluntary.

🏥

Local Study Sites

The REIMAGINE 4 trial is conducted at accredited clinical research sites. Our team will work to connect you with the most conveniently located site in your area to minimise travel burden.

Who May Be Eligible?

The REIMAGINE 4 study is designed for adults who meet specific medical criteria to ensure participant safety and research validity. Review the key eligibility requirements below.

✅

You May Qualify If:

  • You are aged 30 to 70 years
  • You have been diagnosed with Type 2 diabetes for at least 6 months (≥180 days)
  • You are currently on a stable metformin regimen, with or without an SGLT2 inhibitor
  • Your HbA1c level is between 7.0% and 10.5%, indicating suboptimal blood sugar control
  • Your Body Mass Index (BMI) is 30 kg/m² or above
  • You are not pregnant and not currently breastfeeding
❌

You Are Not Eligible If:

  • You are currently pregnant or breastfeeding
  • You have severe renal (kidney) impairment
  • You are currently using insulin therapy
  • You have been diagnosed with Type 1 diabetes
  • You do not meet the BMI or HbA1c thresholds specified above
  • You have other medical conditions that preclude safe participation (a study doctor will assess this)

What to Expect as a Participant

Understanding the study process is important. Here is a clear, step-by-step overview of the participant journey from initial screening through to study completion.

1️⃣

Pre-Screening

Complete our brief online eligibility questionnaire to determine whether you may qualify. This takes approximately 2 minutes and is completely confidential. No medical information is submitted at this stage.

2️⃣

Formal Medical Screening

If you appear eligible, a study coordinator will contact you to arrange a formal medical screening visit at a local study site. This includes blood tests, HbA1c measurement, and a physical assessment.

3️⃣

Informed Consent & Enrolment

If you meet all criteria, a qualified clinician will walk you through the full informed consent process. You will have the opportunity to ask questions before deciding to enrol. Participation is always your choice.

4️⃣

Study Participation

Enrolled participants will receive investigational treatment and attend scheduled clinic visits as per the study protocol. All visits, assessments, and study medications are provided at no cost throughout the trial period.
