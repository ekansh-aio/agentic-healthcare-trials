Who May Be Eligible?

The REIMAGINE 4 study is looking for adults who meet the following general criteria. A full eligibility assessment will be completed by the clinical site team.

  • Adults aged 30 to 70 years
  • Formally diagnosed with Type 2 diabetes
  • Currently taking metformin, with or without an SGLT2 inhibitor
  • HbA1c between 7.0% and 10.5% (inadequate glycaemic control)
  • BMI of 30 or higher
  • Residing within reach of a participating clinical trial site
  • Willing to attend regular scheduled study visits
  • Not currently using insulin therapy
